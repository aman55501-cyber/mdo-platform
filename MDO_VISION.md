## §13 — INTELLIGENCE CLASSIFICATION RULES

Every alert gets classified:

| Level | Trigger | Domain examples |
|---|---|---|
| 🔴 **Critical** | Immediate action today | Compliance overdue, tender <3 days, HIGH urgency intel |
| 🟡 **Important** | Attention this week | Filing in 4–14 days, tender in 4–7 days, competitor wins |
| 🟢 **Info** | No action needed | Market news, completed items |

Every alert MUST include:
1. **What changed** — the actual delta
2. **Why it matters** — financial / risk / strategic impact
3. **Recommended action** — specific, executable, with owner
